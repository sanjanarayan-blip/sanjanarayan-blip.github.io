About

Background

I'm a chemical engineer turned founder, currently building Orbitl, an AI research platform designed for scientists. I graduated from UC Berkeley in May 2026 with a degree in Chemical Engineering.

My path into startups came from seeing firsthand how fragmented and slow research workflows are across universities. I started Orbitl in September 2025 to fix that, starting with customer discovery across multiple universities and building toward a product scientists actually want to use.

I'm part of Antler Residency, SkyDeck PAD-13, and STEP, programs that have sharpened how I think about building, selling, and scaling early-stage companies. I'm drawn to roles at the intersection of GTM, business development, and venture, where I can combine commercial instincts with technical credibility.
