The problem

Scientific research is slow not because scientists lack ability, but because the tools around them are decades behind. Literature review is manual and fragmented. Knowledge stays siloed inside labs and universities. Time that should go toward discovery gets spent on overhead.

What we built

Orbitl gives researchers an AI-powered workspace to surface relevant literature, synthesize findings, and organize their thinking. Designed for how scientists actually work.

GTM motion

Dual-track across 12+ universities. Top-down institutional partnerships and bottom-up researcher adoption. ~12% reply rate cold outbound to R&D directors.

Commercial traction

Pilot conversations at Series A–C deep tech startups. Milestone-based pricing and NDA frameworks structured with industry partners.

Team
