The problem

Scientific research is slow not because scientists lack ability, but because the tools around them are decades behind. Literature review is manual and fragmented. Knowledge stays siloed inside labs and universities. Time that should go toward discovery gets spent on overhead.

What we built

Orbitl gives researchers an AI-powered workspace to surface relevant literature, synthesize findings, and organize their thinking. It is designed for the way scientists actually work, not the way enterprise software assumes they do.

We have conducted extensive customer discovery across multiple universities, built an intern team, and are in active GTM motion targeting research-intensive institutions.

My role

I am the founder. I lead product vision, GTM strategy, customer discovery, and fundraising. My background in chemical engineering gives me direct credibility with our users and informs how we build for scientific workflows.
